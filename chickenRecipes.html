Chicken Goujons

Ingredients:

500g chicken breast
2 eggs
100g flour
100g breadcrumbs

Method:

Step 1: Cut chicken into strips
Step 2: Dip chicken into flour, eggs and breadcrumbs respectively
Step 3: Bake in oven until fully cooked
Step 4: Serve and Enjoy!
