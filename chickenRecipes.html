Chicken Goujons

Crunchy, chicken goujons coated in a 'krispy' batter

Serves 4

  • Ingredients
  • 500g chicken breast
  • 2 eggs
  • 100g flour
  • 100g breadcrumbs

This recipe will take around one hour to make.

  • Method
  • Step 1
    Cut chicken into strips
  • Step 2
    Dip chicken into flour, eggs and breadcrumbs respectively
  • Step 3
    Bake in oven until fully cooked
  • Step 4
    Serve and Enjoy!
