Here is a recommended recipe based on food you have in your fridge currently.

Ingredients:
700ml milk
350g pasta
50g butter
50g plain flour
50g parmesan
Method:
Step 1: Cook the pasta
Step 2: Make a roux, to do this heat butter and flour over heat
Step 3: Stir milk into sauce and simmer until thick and then add the cheese
Step 4: Add pasta and bake for 15-20 mins, the enjoy!
