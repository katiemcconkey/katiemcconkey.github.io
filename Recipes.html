Mac and Cheese

Creamy mac and cheese oven baked to perfection. Perfect for a cold winters day.

Serves 4

  • Ingredients
  • 700ml milk
  • 350g pasta
  • 50g butter
  • 50g plain flour
  • 50g parmesan

This recipe will take around one hour to make.

  • Method
  • Step 1
    Cook the pasta
  • Step 2
    Make a roux, to do this heat butter and flour over heat
  • Step 3
    Stir milk into sauce and simmer until thick and then add the cheese
  • Step 4
    Add pasta and bake for 15-20 mins, then serve and enjoy!
