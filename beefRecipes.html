Beef Stew

This classic French beef stew is the ultimate cold weather comfort food.

Serves 4

  • Ingredients
  • 500g steak chunks
  • 1l Beef Stock
  • 100g Carrots
  • 100g Onions
  • 100g Potatoes

This recipe will take around one day to make.

  • Method
  • Step 1
    Brown beef chunks over high heat
  • Step 2
    Add beef, all veg and stock to a large casserole dish and cook in a low temp in the oven for 6 hours
  • Step 3
    Serve and enjoy!
