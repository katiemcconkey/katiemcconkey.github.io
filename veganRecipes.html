Vegan Quesadilla

Ingredients:

75g Dairy free cheese
2 Large flour tortillas
Sliced jalapenos

Method:

Step 1: Put tortilla into large frying pan and cook over medium heat for 2 minutes
Step 2: Add the cheese, cover with second tortilla and heat until melted
Step 3: Cut into slices and add jalapenos
Step 4: Serve and enjoy!
